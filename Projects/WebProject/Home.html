Elon Musk Website

Elon Musk is an entrepreneur, and a very successful business mann known for his ambitious projects such as SpaceX, Tesla, and Neuralink. Born in South Africa in 1971, Musk moved to the United States to attend college at the University of Pennsylvania. He co-founded Zip2, an online city guide software company, which was later sold for over $300 million. Musk then went on to co-found X.com, which eventually became PayPal, before moving on to found SpaceX, Tesla, and other companies.

Musk is widely recognized for his goal of colonizing Mars, his efforts to revolutionize transportation with electric cars and high-speed trains, and his advocacy for renewable energy and sustainable living.
